MacBeath is a family of themes which includes **MacBeath** (Front end theme), **MacKart** (E-Commerce @ Shopping cart theme), **MacAdmin** (Admin theme). It has more than **70+ pages and comes in 6 Cool colors**. Take a look at full list of features below. 

###Font End Features:

 1. 3 Cool sliders.
 2. Fully Responsive.
 3. 3 Home page layouts.
 4. 4 Blog layouts.
 5. More than 40 pages.
 6. 6 cool colors.
 7. Responsive refine slider for recent post.
 8. Flex slider for images, testimonials and about section.
 9. Black and White photo effect.
 10. Gallery with isotope effect.
 11. Filter script for Support page.
 12. Cool resume page.
 13. Login and Register forms.
 14. Coming soon section with twitter feed.
 15. Twitter script, Google web fonts and font awesome icons.
 16. It supports almost all features of twitter bootstrap. 

###E-Commerce Features:

 1. Flex Slider.
 2. 6 Colors & 18 Pages.
 3. Blog page.
 4. Responsive recent item slider.
 5. Colorful promo box.
 6. Cool sidebar navigation.
 7. Review section.
 8. Support page (filtering effect).
 9. Login and Register page (2 styles).
 10. Complete account pages (wish list, order summary, profile edit, etc.,).
 11. Checkout page and view cart page. 

###Admin Features:

 1. Cool charts and graphs.
 2. Sidebar navigation with dropdown menu. 
 3. Lots of Cool widgets.
 4. Calendar support.
 5. Colorful sliders. 
 6. Login, Register and Support page. 
 7. Lots of UI Tools.
 8. Table support. .
 9. Professional and Clean User Interface. 
 10. More than 10 html files.
